How to Add Events to Your Live Website

1

Add your event here

Click Events & News in the sidebar, then Add New. Fill in the title, date, type, and description.

2

Download events.json

Click the green Download events.json button. This saves a file to your computer with all your events.

3

Open Hostinger File Manager

Log in to hpanel.hostinger.com → Hosting → Manage → File Manager.

4

Navigate to public_html

Open the public_html folder. This is where your website files live.

5

Upload & Replace

Click Upload, select the events.json you downloaded. If asked to replace, click Yes.

6

Done! ✓

Refresh your website. The new events appear immediately on the public Events section — visible to all donors and visitors.

Tip: The events.json file in your admin panel is just a local editor. The live website always reads from the file on Hostinger. Always upload after making changes.
